What You ACTUALLY Need On Your Baby Registry

A simple, realistic guide to help you sort what is essential, what is optional, and what is usually not worth the money.

What's actually essential

  • A safe sleep space
  • A properly installed car seat
  • A small batch of simple clothes and sleepers
  • Diapering basics
  • A feeding setup that matches your current plan
  • Postpartum recovery basics

What's optional

  • Extra nursery organizers
  • Swings, bouncers, and specialty gear
  • Warmers and convenience gadgets
  • Lots of newborn outfits

What's a waste of money

  • Multiple versions of the same product before baby is here
  • Decor-heavy products that do not improve daily life
  • Single-use gadgets that solve a problem you may never have

Budget vs premium

  • Spend more where safety and long-term use matter most
  • Keep basics basic when they do the job well
  • Start with less and add later if needed

Partner section

  • Make sure both of you understand the setup
  • Choose products that feel easy to use when tired
  • Plan for postpartum as a team, not just baby gear
